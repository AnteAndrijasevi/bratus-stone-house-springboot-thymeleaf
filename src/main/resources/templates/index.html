Your Perfect Getaway

Our beautifully renovated stone house combines traditional architecture with modern comfort, offering an authentic Dalmatian experience.

Completely renovated in 2021, this charming home preserves its historic character while providing all the contemporary amenities you need for a comfortable and memorable stay. Located in the peaceful village of Bratuš, just 5 km south of Baška Voda, and nestled at the foot of the impressive Biokovo mountain range.

With the pebble beach only steps away, you can enjoy the crystal clear Adriatic waters anytime you wish.
